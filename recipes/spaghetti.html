Spaghetti bolognese

Description

Heat a pan with the oil and add slice onion. When fried, add mince beef. Add salt and pepper. After the meat is done, mix it with tomato sauce and suitable spices.

Couple minutes before adding tomato sauce, boil up water and cook the spaghetti. When both is finished, serve with grated cheese.

Ingredients

  • spaghetti
  • onion
  • mince beef
  • salt, pepper and other spices
  • tomato sauce

Steps

  1. fry sliced onion
  2. add mince beef and roast it
  3. mix it in tomato sauce and flavor it
  4. serve with grated cheese
